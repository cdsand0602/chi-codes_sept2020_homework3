Musician, Rock Star, His Royal Badness (1958 - 2016)

10 Things About Prince

  • He was born epilectic and had seizures when he was young.
  • He wrote his first song when he was 7. It was named "Funk Machine" and was written on his father's piano.
  • He could play at least 27 instruments.
  • He signed his record deal with Warner Bros at 19. He later fought the compnay for creative control. He changed his name to an unpronounceable symbol and became known at "The Artist Formerly Known as Prince" during this time.
  • He played all the instruments for all the songs on his first album except one.
  • He was rushed off the stage as opening act for the Rolling Stones as he performed in a trench coat and bikini briefs.
  • He required his management teal to obtain a movie deal for him and Purple Rain was born.
  • The Purple Rain album sold more than 13 million copies in the US alone.
  • Andy Worhol was fascinated by Prince and create 12 unique paintings of him.
  • Prince spent the latter part of his career advocating for artists to fight for creative control and artistic freedom.
